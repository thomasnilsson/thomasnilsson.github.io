About

I am software engineer, currently employed by The Copenhagen Center of Health Technology, and have been involved in Health Tech since 2017 when I started the company Edea IT with a fellow student. Through the company we developed which provided web- and mobile solutions for patient studies in hospital clinics.

In the spring of 2020 I went abroad to TUM and 2019 I spent an exchange semester at ETH Zurich.

Master's Thesis

I received my Master's degree in Computer Science from the Technical University of Denmark in July 2020. The thesis is available here: A Flutter Package for Real-time Mobility Feature Extraction.

The Mobility Features Flutter package has been released on the dart package manager.

My supervisor was Jakob Bardram, the director of CACHET.
